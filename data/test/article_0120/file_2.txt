A virtual testing ground for P1 system features is available for anyone interested in exploring it before its official launch alongside Period 105's call for proposals cycle begins! This free space allows you to draft complete proposals using all La Silla Paranal Observatory's instruments along with various observation program types like normal or target observation modes within various timeframes like short or long duration observations.. The platform provides clear guidance through instructional boxes offering concise explanations about each process involved while also offering detailed descriptions on demand through mini-help sections . While many aspects mirror ESOFORM structure familiar from past submissions , additional support resources can be accessed via menus or buttons throughout your interface experience . For those planning their own future submissions , we highly recommend familiarizing yourself with this platform now as significant changes have been implemented regarding how runs are defined compared to previous versions .
