The E-ELT Design Reference Mission and Science Plan workshop took place at ESO in Garching, Germany, from May 26 to 28, 2009. Using intended research efforts for the European Extremely Large Telescope (E-ELT), the workshop focused on updating the community on the E-ELT's status and particularly on developing the science case through simulations. It brought together various stakeholders, such as instrument study teams and scientists from a working group and the E-ELT Science Office, to facilitate open discussions and evaluation of E-ELT performance simulation results. Additionally, discussions regarding the E-ELT Design Reference Science Plan (DRSP) were held.
