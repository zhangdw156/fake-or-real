The intended plan for an expansion of the ESO Headquarters building was carefully crafted taking into account potential future growth and improvements to current facilities, leading to a request for ideas to be sent to 24 architectural firms in ESO member states on 11 July 2007, with a submission deadline of 24 September. An international jury, comprised of distinguished architects as well as representatives from the town of Garching and ESO, evaluated the submitted designs. They received twenty responses and awarded two first prizes, one third prize, and one fourth prize in October 2007. One notably innovative design, despite not following local building regulations, was recognized with an award for creativity.
