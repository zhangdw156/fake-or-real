This document outlines European Southern Observatory's (ESO) future plans in astronomy over both this year's decade ahead and beyond that timeframe.. It details their operations across various telescopes like those at La Silla Paranal Observatory; ALMA APEX; the upcoming Extremely Large Telescope (ELT); along with their planned expansion into multi-messenger astronomy through hosting a Cherenkov Telescope Array at Paranal . It also discusses staffing development within ESO's long term financial plan while highlighting Chile's vital role in its operations . Finally it explores potential new opportunities alongside challenges faced by ESO to help define its path forward as an organization .
